Vote Remain on 23rd May

On the 23rd of May, the UK will probably be taking part in the European election. This will elect the European Parliament for the next five years.

In this election, we ask for you to vote for a party that supports remaining in the European Union.

The more people do this, the less likely Brexit is to happen. If enough people do this, we kill Brexit stone dead.

More details will appear on this website as they become known.
